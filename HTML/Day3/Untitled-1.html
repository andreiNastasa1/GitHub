Where to find video games

The best place to find video games is on an application called Steam. Steam is a video game distributor which sells all genres of games from fighting and adventure to puzzle and simulators.

I have games but no game

One thing you might notice as you play more games is that you have no life. You don't have friends let alone a girlfriend. How do you get out of your cave as a gamer?

The simple answer is conventions! Gaming/Anime conventions happen all the time in all parts of the world. Here's a video of some cringe stuff you can do to appeal the masses (or more likely lose all dignity).

How can I find a convention?

You can look for conventions online by just typing "gaming convention" on Google. There are multiple websites centered around gaming culture which frequently post about the latest conventions that are happening.

Getting to the convention is a whole other story. You can use google maps below to search where the convention you're looking for is. Make sure to start preparing to go there multiple weeks in advance and double or even triple check how you will get there and how you will return to your cave.

Become a God Gamer

So you know how to play find games, and win at the game of life, but something is missing. You only become a true gamer when you achieve something.

Speedrunning

What is speedrunning? Speedrunning is trying to complete a game as fast as possible. The speedrunning scene is very competitive but if you manage to get on the leaderboards, you will gain the atmost respect from all gamers. And that is how you become a God Gamer.
